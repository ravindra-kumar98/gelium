About Us

The Business Behind the Products You Know and Love

Gelium Lubricants – Where Trust Meets Performance.

With 18 years of industry experience, Gelium Lubricants has built a reputation for trust, quality, and innovation in the lubricant and grease market. From our humble beginnings to becoming a leading manufacturer, we have consistently delivered top-tier products that meet the diverse needs of our customers. Our extensive product range includes industrial and automotive lubricants and greases, engineered to enhance performance and protection.

We believe that strong relationships are the foundation of success. Our trusted network of partners and distributors spans across India, enabling us to deliver high-quality products wherever they are needed. We value these relationships, continuously striving to provide exceptional service and reliable solutions.

Gelium Cummins firmly believes that its growth potential lies in the quality of execution, power to innovate and in its resolve to remain a step ahead of market demands. Its mission as a brand is to leverage real-world insights to provide valuable tools that generate success for its customers and users.
